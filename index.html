About us

What is zkDiggers? 

ZkDiggers is a groundbreaking NFT collection that explores the intersection of art and blockchain technology. Featuring a series of meticulously designed digital artworks, ZkDiggers stands out with its unique mining-themed visuals and innovative use of zero-knowledge proofs for enhanced privacy and security.
